THE STORY
IN A NUTSHELL
A famous professor had been doing experiments with squirrels. Squirrels have died in the process. When their friends found out...
Almost immediately severe squirrel attacks surged across the globe. It's the first time in modern history that humankind has faced absolute extinction...
The Global Squirrel Council reaches out to you - a group of young inventors - with an offer...
Do your part of the deal, bring back peace to Earth and save humankind.
Fail the trust of squirrels and... there will be no human left on Earth to tell about your failure. Ready?
