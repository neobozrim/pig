HOW TO PLAY
GOAL
Bring the most technological and societal advancement to the world of squirrels, become the New Squirrel Idol, save humankind from complete extinction and lead the squirrel tech revolution. Clear enough? Great.
HOW LONG IS IT?
30 minutes on average. We wanted a short and dynamic game that makes friends want to have "another one" right after the first game.
TWO DECKS
There are Regular cards and Special cards. All played cards go the Scrap Yard.
REGULAR CARDS
Here follow the cards from the latest prototype. PLEASE NOTE that card design is going to vastly improve in the coming months.
There are 3 groups of inventions: Education, Technology and Life Sciences.
INVENTIONS: a single invention that is still operational at the end of the game brings 1 point. Two inventions of the same group bring 3 points, three inventions of the same group bring 4 points. Broken inventions don't bring points at the end of the game.
BREAK: you can break other players' inventions.
FIX: you can fix your broken inventions with the help of the God of Tape Fixes.
RUMOUR: when you draw a rumour card - immediately place it in the middle of the table. It's an active rumour that some of you has treated squirrels badly.
SQUIRREL ATTACK: three active rumours unlock a Squirrel Attack! If you can't fend off the attack, each player loses an already created invention. Fail to fend off three attacks during the game and YOU LOSE THE GAME AND FAIL HUMANITY... So you better team up and fend off the attacks.
SQUIRRELS DON'T ATTACK SQUIRRELS: collectively players have to contribute a total of three such cards in order to fend off a Squirrel Attack. Everybody's free to contribute as many such cards as they wish.
TAKE A BREAK: you skip the next two turns.
EARTHQUAKE: tectonic plates move and the player who drew the card has to decide who's invention moves to the posession of another player.
FIRE: during a fire two cards from the hand of each player burn and go to the Scrap Yard without anybody knowing what they were.
FLOOD: your inventor labs are flooded and each player salvages the inventions they have on hand at the time by creating them in the last moment - all broken.
SPECIAL CARDS
SQUIRREL BRAIN POWER: have a psycho squirrel use their brain power to protect you from a successful squirrel attack. You don't lose any inventions, but pick another player who looses an additional invention instead of you.
STEAL: steal another player's invention - be it operation or broken. Broken inventions remain broken after having been stollen.
PROTECT: protect your inventions when somebody attempts to steal or break them.
BORROW A CARD: draw a card from the hand of another player of your choice. The card is now yours. It may not be fair to say you are borrowing it, but what can you do?
SKIP A TURN: make a player skip a turn. Play this card way before their turn or at least before they've made any actions during their turn.
RECOVER: recover any one card that has been discarded in the scrap yard. Everybody should see what you're recovering.
SNEAK A PEAK: secretively take a look at the next one card in both decks - Regular and Special. If there are no Special cards left, you could take a look at the top two Regular cards.
SQUIRREL BRIGADE: protect yourself from either Earthquake, Flood or Fire by employing a Squirrel Brigade.
INITIAL SETUP
Each player starts with 6 cards - 5 Regular cards and 1 Special card. No "Play Immediately" cards are allowed in a players hand at the start of the game.
The oldest player starts first and then turns move anti-clockwise (the direction the old Bulgarian dance "horo" moves).
RULES
You can create only 1 invention on your turn, by placing it face up in front of you. You can play an unlimited number of other cards during your turn.   Your turn finishes with drawing a card. The game ends when the Regular cards deck finishes. When the last regular card has been drawn - each player has one final turn. The player who has collected the most points for their inventions wins the game.
A player's turn finishes with drawing a card from either of both decks - Regular cards or Special cards. In order to be able to draw a Special card you need to have at least two operational inventions created.
FINAL WORDS
