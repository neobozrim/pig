About the game

Squirrel Attack is a board game inspired by the squirrels at the Michigan State University campus and created in Sofia, Bulgaria. You and your fellow inventors compete with each other creating inventions that transfer the progress of humans to the lives of squirrels. The one who does best becomes the New Squirrel Idol... unless you collectively lose the game and thus bring an end to humankind. Do you have what it takes to become the New Squirrel Idol?
